About Us

Island Central Mactan - I.T. Complex, a mixed-used development, inaugurated in 2016, is a dynamic mixed-use development strategically positioned within the Mactan Economic Zone in Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu, Philippines.

As a key player in the region, it seamlessly integrates Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) facilities with diverse retail offerings, catering to both local and international markets. With its prime location right across the Mactan Cebu International Airport, it offers well balanced stunning views of Cebu City, and the pristine beaches of Lapu Lapu City.

Island Central Mactan goes beyond conventional shopping, delivering a vibrant and comprehensive lifestyle experience that embodies the unique charm of island living.
